Hi there, I´m Bodoni

I am a font that was named and created by the Italian type designer and punchcutter Giambattista Bodoni (born in 1740) his father and grandfather were both printmakers. Bodoni used his design expertise to create me with the intention of sophistication. I´m often characterized by my sharp contrast of thin and thick strokes. This conveys my clean and classic nature, which makes me a timeless font that will never go out of style. As a font, I´m mostly attractive at larger sizes where you can see the detailes clearly such as headlines, poster-displays and logos. I am a popular choice for high-end brands, especially in the field of fashion. I´m the signature typeface of VOGUE magazine and ELLE. Other brands such as Calvin Klein and Armani use me as a foundation for their branding.

References: Kara Perilli
