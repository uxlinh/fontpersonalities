Hello world! I´m Roboto

I am a font created by the interface designer Christian Robertson at Google. As a font I have a dual nature. I have a mechanical skeleton and my forms are largely geometric. At the same time, I feature friendly and open curves. While some grotesks distort their letterforms to force a rigid rhythm, I don’t compromise, I allow letters to be settled into their natural width. This makes for a more natural reading rhythm more commonly found in humanist and serif types.
